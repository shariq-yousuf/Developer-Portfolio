Change Champ

  Tired of manually calculating change and keeping track of your cash drawer? Introducing Change Champ, your one-stop shop for efficient and accurate cash register management! This handy app not only calculates the exact change you owe customers based on your current drawer balance, but it also tracks your transactions and provides detailed breakdowns of your coin inventory.

How to Use:

Set Up Your Drawer:

  • Open the app and start by setting up your cash drawer.
  • Simply enter the amount of each coin and bill you have on hand.
  • Click "Save" to lock this information and ensure accurate change calculations. Need to adjust your drawer later? No problem! Click "Edit" to update your cash amounts at any time.

Add Items:

  • Once your drawer is set, enter the cost of each item the customer has purchased.
  • You can add items one by one for more complex transactions, or enter the total amount for a quick checkout.

Calculate Change with Confidence:

  • Enter the amount of cash the customer pays.
  • With a tap of the "Calculate Change" button, the app takes over!
  • It analyzes your drawer balance and displays the exact change you can return, ensuring you always have the necessary denominations available.

Additional Features: (coming soon)

  • Track your sales history for better inventory management.
  • Export transaction data for easy record-keeping.
  • Manage multiple cash drawers (perfect for businesses with multiple registers).

Say goodbye to checkout headaches and hello to a smoother, more efficient way to handle cash transactions!
